Purpose of This Website

This website was created to educate students about artificial intelligence, helping them understand what AI is, how it works, and most importantly, how to use it safely and responsibly. As AI becomes increasingly integrated into our daily lives and educational environments, it's crucial for students to learn about both its benefits and potential risks.

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a technology that allows computers and machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. AI systems can learn from data, recognize patterns, make decisions, and solve problems.

AI is already part of our daily lives through examples like chatbots that answer questions, voice assistants like Siri or Alexa, recommendation systems on streaming platforms, and learning tools that adapt to student needs.

Understanding AI helps students make informed decisions about how to use these powerful tools safely and effectively.
